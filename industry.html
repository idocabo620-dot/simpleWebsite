Beauty Industry & Cybersecurity

How makeup and skincare brands protect customer data

Values Beauty Cybersecurity

Industry Overview

The beauty industry includes makeup and skincare brands that sell products online. These companies store customer accounts, purchase history, and shipping details.

Why Cybersecurity Matters

Because beauty brands process online payments, they are common targets for cyberattacks. Hackers may steal login credentials or payment data.

Common Cyber Threats

• Phishing emails
• Credential stuffing attacks
• Fake brand websites
• Checkout skimming malware
• Third-party vendor breaches

Best Cybersecurity Practices

• Secure payment processors
• Encryption of customer data
• Multi-factor authentication
• Fraud detection systems
• Regular website updates

Consequences of Poor Security

Weak cybersecurity can result in stolen data, financial losses, and loss of customer trust. Once trust is lost, customers may stop buying from the brand.
