Personal Values

My top four values and why they matter to me

Values Beauty Cybersecurity

Integrity

Description: Being honest and doing the right thing even when no one is watching.

Origin: My upbringing and learning that trust must be earned.

Importance: Builds trust and credibility.

Without it: Loss of trust, damaged relationships, and missed opportunities.

Continuous Learning

Description: Staying curious and improving my skills.

Origin: School and real-life experiences.

Importance: Keeps me adaptable and prepared.

Without it: Falling behind and missing opportunities.

Respect

Description: Treating others with dignity.

Origin: Family values and the golden rule.

Importance: Builds strong relationships.

Without it: Conflict and broken relationships.

Responsibility

Description: Taking ownership of my actions.

Origin: Being held accountable growing up.

Importance: Makes me reliable.

Without it: Loss of trust and credibility.
